# Direction Template

```markdown
# Direction: {Topic}

**From Brainstorming:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Selected Option:** {option name}

## Problem Statement

{One paragraph: what we're solving and why}

## Selected Approach

{Description of chosen option from evaluation}

## Research Questions (if going to research)

1. {specific question to answer}
2. {specific question to answer}
3. {specific question to answer}

## Requirements (if going to planning)

- **R1:** {requirement from brainstorming}
- **R2:** {requirement}

## Known Constraints

- {constraint from brainstorming}
- {constraint}

## Success Criteria

- {how we'll know we're done}
- {measurable outcome}
```

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Problem statement is one paragraph, not a list
- [ ] Selected approach references the option from evaluation
- [ ] Either Research Questions OR Requirements is filled (not both)
- [ ] Constraints are things that can't change
- [ ] Success criteria are verifiable
